In the context of the ASEAN Economic Community will be formed at the end of this year, as well as many important free trade agreements (FTAs) that Vietnam has been and are about to sign, it is required to innovate technology and raise High quality products to increase competitiveness is an important issue of Vietnam plastic industry.


This information is mentioned at the Conference "Development orientation for Vietnam plastic industry - welcoming opportunities from the formation of ASEAN economic community", organized by Vietnam Chamber of Commerce and Industry (VCCI) in the afternoon of 16th. / 9, in Ho Chi Minh City (Ho Chi Minh City).


According to Dr. Dinh The Hien, Director of Ho Chi Minh City Applied Economics Research Institute, the biggest weakness of Vietnam plastic industry is backward production technology. Because Vietnam's products are in the low segment, businesses pay less attention to investing in modern machinery technology. This reduces the competitiveness of Vietnamese plastic products in the market. However, in the context of integration, the more worrisome issue is the management and leadership capacity of plastic enterprises, not determined enough to innovate, create and apply new product technologies.


Mr. Nguyen Thanh Binh, Director of VCCI Trade Information Center, said that there are more than 80% of plastic enterprises with small and medium scale. There is no group of enterprises holding the leading position to lead the plastic industry so the competitiveness within the industry is quite high. Besides, the concentration of small and medium enterprises in the same field also means that the capital and technology of enterprises are quite limited, so they will face strong competitive pressure against enterprises with direct investment capital. Foreign reception (FDI), which has advantages in technology and market experience. Meanwhile, plastic industry in some countries in ASEAN region such as Thailand has welcomed the trend of world consumption by aiming to produce biotech-friendly and environmentally friendly plastic products.


According to VCCI, Vietnam's plastic industry still has certain limitations such as domestic production materials only meet 20% to 30% of demand with products mainly including PVC, PET, PP. The remaining 70% of the industry's raw materials are dependent on imports. Therefore, production costs are always fluctuated according to the price of input materials from the world and fluctuations of foreign exchange rates. Meanwhile, the number of models and types of plastic products is monotonous, there are not many value-added products as well as failing to meet the increasing demands of the market. The domestic plastic industry has not had any research facilities and technological and technological inventions, the workers are not highly skilled and have no workers training system, so they cannot produce high details. grant to participate in global supply chain in a fixed position ...


Some businesses in the plastic industry said that the Vietnamese plastic industry currently lacks a team of experts with deep knowledge of the industry, can analyze and provide information on the world plastic market. Enterprises also find it difficult to access industry-related data and information to plan long-term development strategies. According to the recommendations of the plastic industry enterprises, the Ministry of Industry and Trade as well as the Vietnam Plastic Association need to have a close link in making analysis data, forecasting the situation of plastic production and export in the country as well as The world, so that businesses in the industry can come up with long-term development strategies, keep abreast of the situation and actual needs from the market.
